This is such a great series and so are the books. I definitely like the newer Percy Jackson better than the ones made in the 2010's. Recently a new season came out. I haven't watched it yet but I'm so excited! If you haven't read the series, something's wrong with you. The main character Percy has ADHD and dislexia, I feel like I really connected to the character because I also have dyslexia. When reading this for the first time I really saw myself in Percy because I also struggled with school and trying to concentrate.
