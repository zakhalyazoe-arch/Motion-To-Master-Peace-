When I was watching Damsel my jaw was literally dropped the whole time during the movie. It was like plot twist after plot twist, and the SGI was absolutely awesome. When I was looking for an image to draw I found this one. You don't know how excited I was to draw this because of how awesome it looked. Overall I really recommend this movie to anyone who enjoys action and adventure.

Drawing Damsel was super fun and really tricky, I honestly thought it would be easier to draw but I was definitely lying. Sometimes when my drawing is not working out and doesn't look good I put my own drawing style into it. The weird thing about having a specific art style is it changes as you grow and as you learn more drawing skills, that's why my drawings sometimes look completely different from each other.
